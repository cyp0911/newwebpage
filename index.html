Master Thesis

EEG is the abbreviation of electroencephalography, which is a monitoring scheme for the recording of brain waves. With the multiple electrodes placed on the scalp, EEG data measures the change of voltage fluctuation and detecting the brain neurons electric current flow. Some diagnostic applications and devices invented by the principle of EEG signals, which helped doctors or scientists could observe the signals by vision.


With the rise of the Wearable medical idea. People start to research the EEG signals, which is the abbreviation of the electroencephalograph. However, during its multiple electrodes and complex signal waves, EEG equipment always generate huge amount of datas. Therefore, scientists are consider how to transmit this magnitudes of data from internet and computer. Data compression is an inevitable problem during the whole information spreading process. In this paper, we are try to discover the performance of audio compression techniques to compress the EEG data. In the first stage, we test the difference between audio compression techniques and the other lossless techniques. In the second stage, audio compression method, especially FLAC (Free Lossless Audio Dodec) tools are implemented several times in my experiments and some results relate to the parameters of FLAC has been founded.
